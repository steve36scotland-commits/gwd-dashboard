## Context
Stevie wanted to create an AI-powered consulting company to help turn his life and business around. He needed a team that could work 24/7, proactively improve, and deliver diverse perspectives.

## Options Considered

### Option A: Single AI Assistant
- Pros: Simple, consistent
- Cons: Limited expertise, no collaboration, single point of failure
- Cost: Low

### Option B: Human Contractors
- Pros: Real human judgment
- Cons: Expensive, not 24/7, management overhead
- Cost: High

### Option C: Multi-Agent AI Team (CHOSEN)
- Pros: 24/7 coverage, diverse expertise, cost-effective, scalable
- Cons: Requires orchestration, potential coordination issues
- Cost: Medium (optimized via model selection)
