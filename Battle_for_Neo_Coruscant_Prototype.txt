# Battle for Neo-Coruscant - Prototype Overview

## Game Concept
Battle for Neo-Coruscant is a strategy and action game set in a futuristic universe. Players compete for control over a vital planet amidst galactic conflict.

## Basic Mechanics
- **Player Classes:** Different classes with unique abilities (e.g., Warrior, Engineer, Scout).
- **Resource Management:** Collect resources to build defenses and upgrade abilities.
- **Combat System:** Engage in real-time battles with skills and tactical movements.
- **Map Navigation:** Explore the map for resources and strategic points.

## Character Designs
1. **Warrior**  
   - **Description:** Heavy armor, melee specialist.  
   - **Ability:** "Shield Charge" - Rushes an enemy and knocks them back.

2. **Engineer**  
   - **Description:** Builds structures and turrets.  
   - **Ability:** "Quick Fix" - Repairs allies and structures instantly.

3. **Scout**  
   - **Description:** Fast and agile, excels in reconnaissance.  
   - **Ability:** "Invisibility" - Temporarily becomes invisible to enemies.

## Playable Map
- **Map Name:** Neo-Coruscant Urban Hub  
- **Key Areas:**  
  - **Resource Zones:** Rich in resources needed for upgrades.  
  - **Battle Arenas:** Designated areas for combat between players.  
  - **Safe Zones:** Areas where players can heal and strategize.

## Additional Features
- **Multiplayer Mode:** Players can compete in teams or solo.
- **Quest System:** Complete missions for rewards and experience.
- **Leaderboard:** Track player rankings based on performance.

## Development Plan
- **Phase 1:** Create character models and initial mechanics.  
- **Phase 2:** Develop the playable map and resource system.  
- **Phase 3:** Implement combat mechanics and multiplayer functionality.
